Virtual Diabetologist
Treatment Oriented Decision An EU-funded medical decision platform bringing specialist expertise to family physicians. Developed with diabetologists, it analyzes patient data and generates personalized therapy recommendations with automated HZZO compliance. The system reduces time to treatment while preventing costly complications transforming diabetes care for over 310,000 patients in Croatia.
Digital Freight Forwarder
Intelligent Document Automation A comprehensive freight forwarding system that automates document generation including AWB/HAWB, freight documents and barcode labels. Built-in profit calculators and chargeable weight algorithms optimize pricing, while code libraries and validation controls minimize manual entry. The application enables freight forwarders to process complex shipments with enhanced accuracy and operational efficiency.
